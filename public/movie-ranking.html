My Top 3 Movies of All Time

The Royal Tenenbaums

Family is a sentence. Really captures how people from complicated families feel about each other. And it's hilarious.

Mad Max: Fury Road

Literally the best action movie ever. If you haven't seend it, you need to.

Moana

It's a film about leaving home, finding what you are meant to do, and saving the world.
